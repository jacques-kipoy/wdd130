White Water Rafting Trip Description

1. Colorado River, Grand Canyon

Description: Experience the thrill of rafting down the Colorado River through the majestic Grand Canyon. This trip offers breathtaking scenery,
challenging rapids, and the chance to explore hidden beaches

Highlights:

  • Navigate through iconic rapids like Lava Falls and crystal Rapid
  • Enjoy stunning vistas of the canyon walls and starry nights
  • Camp along the riverbanks under the stars

What to expect:

  • Guided trips with safety briefings and equipment provided
  • Delicious riverside meals prepared by your guides

2. Salmon River, Idaho

Description: The Salmon River offers a mix of adrenaline-pumping rapids and tranquil stretches. Perfect for both beginners and experienced rafters, this trip showcases Idaho's stunning wilderness.

Highlights:

  • Variety of Rapids: Experience everything from Class II to Class V rapids.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Spot eagles, deer, and other wildlife along the banks.
  • Hot Springs: Relax in natural hot springs after a day on the river.

What to Expect:

  • Guided trips with safety briefings and equipment provided.
  • Delicious riverside meals prepared by your guides.

3. Gauley River, West Virginia

Description: Known for its challenging rapids, the Gauley River is a must-visit for thrill-seekers. This trip is particularly popular during the fall when the water levels are at their peak.

Highlights:

  • Famous Rapids: Tackle legendary rapids like Sweet's Falls and Pillow Rock.
  • Scenic Beauty: Enjoy the stunning autumn foliage along the riverbanks.
  • Adventure Packages: Combine rafting with zip-lining and rock climbing.

What to Expect:

  • Professional guides trained in swift water rescue.
  • All necessary safety gear and equipment included.
